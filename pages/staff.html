Tommy Ludeke
Groomer / Owner

Starting as a vet tech in Florida, I went into grooming 4 years ago and worked for a few different shops and salons. I decided to open my own business and bring the salon to you! My main goal of the business is to give your pets a stress free grooming experience. I care about animals and want to help them in any way I can, cage free one on one grooming in the comfort of their own homes.
